# stats220
This is my repo for STATS 220. 

A little about me:

- I am undertaking a degree of Science, specialising in Data Science, and I am in my second year of study. Most of my life right now revolves around datasets, assignments, and trying to convince my laptop not to crash when I run code :)
- I am taking STATS 220 because I want to learn how to actually do cool and useful things with data using R, not just open a dataset, stare at it for five minutes, and hope the answers reveal themselves. - I am interested in learning about data visualisation, automating data processes, and turning messy real-world data into something understandable. I am also excited to see how different data technologies can work together to solve problems. Hopefully by the end of this course, I'll be able to make graphs that look impressive enough that people won't notice how long it took me to write the code.
